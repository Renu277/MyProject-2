Oulu:These are some of our favourite things:

Long sandy shoreline of the Nallikari beach and the Market Square’s jolly spirits on a balmy summer day. Multitude of events for all tastes, splendid restaurant selection, and cozy cafes. The sea ice that lures you outdoors and well-tended ski tracks, cycling along scenic routes all year round.

Wild nature meets urban living

Easy-going hosts warmly welcome you to explore our beautiful city: to the sunny, lively seaside city with a multitude of events, to autumn’s culture selection, to white winter snows or to a blooming spring when nature blossoms. Mode of transport is up to you alone.
